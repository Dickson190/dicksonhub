The answer to that question is a big NO. You cannot create more than one account, as doing so will cause both accounts to be DEACTIVATED PERMANENTLY. All earnings and deposits will not be refunded or paid out.

We process withdrawals within 6 hours to 48 hours to check for fraudulent activities. Very soon, withdrawals will be as swift as possible. If after 48 hours you did not receive payment, ensure you file a REPORT.

How to Get Claim Codes

Claim codes are codes that, once claimed, credit one with ₦50 - ₦700 for free. Claim codes are posted once a week on our social media platforms. Users with Gold or Premium memberships have early access to these codes.

How to Start Earning

Welcome to DicksonHub! To start earning, simply follow these steps:

  1. Register and create an account.
  2. Log in to your account and navigate to the Earn Now page.
  3. Follow the instructions to start earning by completing tasks, claiming codes, or participating in offers.
  4. Track your earnings on your dashboard and withdraw your earnings when you meet the minimum threshold.
How to Withdraw Your Earnings

Once you've accumulated enough earnings, you can withdraw your funds:

  1. Go to your Profile page and select the withdrawal option from the side menu.
  2. Choose your preferred withdrawal method: Airtime or Bank Transfer.
  3. Enter the required details and the amount you wish to withdraw.
  4. Submit your request and wait for the confirmation. Your funds will be processed and transferred accordingly.
